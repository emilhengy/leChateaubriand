About us

Nicolas Dupont is bringing his french cuisine and traditions to New Zeland's capital. For monsieur Dupont, food has always been a way to gather people. He loves cooking for his friends and familly and bring people togheter through food. "For me food has always been what gathers friends and familly together. French lifestyle is all about that. Inviting friends home, share a good meal, drinking good wine og endless discutions around the table about life is what makes the beauty of french gastronomie. Putting a smile on peoples faces through my dishes is what I'm aiming for when I cook. Food is not just for human survival, it's magical too".
